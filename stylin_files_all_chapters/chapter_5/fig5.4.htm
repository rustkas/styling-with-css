A Simple Two-Column Layout

Step 4 - a few styles later. Now the layout looks like someone actually designed it.

  • The top and bottom borders of navigation div are set to blue, each with different line weights.
  • The nav links have increased vertical spacing and are better positioned within the div.
  • The content has left and right padding to move it away from the edge of the browser.
